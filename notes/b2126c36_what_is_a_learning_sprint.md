# b2126c36 What is a learning sprint?

In self-directed, ["agile" learning](961174eb_agile_learning.md) who are the stakeholders and how do you demonstrate the value of what has been accomplished in a [sprint](3ddb2bf8_sprints_are_for_fast_feedback.md)? The only stakeholder is clearly oneself. But what is a demonstrable unit of learning? Wearing the agile team hat, how does one demonstrate a unit of progress? Wearing the stakeholder hat, how does one judge the demo?
